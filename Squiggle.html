Squiggle


Traverse procedurally generated worlds collecting items and points using your finger to draw platforms which your character runs on. Items collected can be spent to increase the diversity of the worlds generated in each run as well as change what you draw and how your character looks.

Compete with your friends with integrated Google Play Achievements and Leaderboards to see who is the best Squiggler out there!
